## Teacher Note

Customize this letter with your school name, dates, and personal details. The goal is to inform families about what their child will be doing and why it's safe. You can send this as a printed letter, email, or include it in your weekly classroom communication. A tear-off permission slip is included at the bottom for schools that require formal consent.

## The Letter

[SCHOOL NAME]  
[TEACHER NAME]  
[DATE]

Dear Parent or Guardian,

I'm writing to tell you about an exciting learning project we're starting in [GRADE/CLASS]. Over the next six weeks, your child will be working with **Are-Self**, a free AI learning tool that will help them explore math, science, writing, history, and other subjects in new ways.

### What Is Are-Self?

Are-Self is a free, open-source AI assistant that runs right here on our school computer. Think of it as a smart learning partner—like having a tutor who can explain concepts, answer questions, and help students work through problems. Unlike ChatGPT or other AI tools you might have heard about, Are-Self runs completely on our school's own hardware. It doesn't connect to the internet. It doesn't store student names or personal information. It doesn't show ads or track students. It's built entirely for learning in a classroom setting.

### What Will Your Child Be Doing?

Your child will use Are-Self across several subjects to:
- Ask questions and get explanations of tricky concepts
- Brainstorm ideas for writing and projects
- Solve math problems step-by-step
- Research historical events and scientific topics
- Get feedback on their thinking

Students will work in pairs or small groups. I'll guide the whole process, and I'll have full control over what Are-Self can do, what it can access, and what conversations are saved.

### Why Is This Safe?

I understand if you have questions about AI in the classroom. Here's why I'm confident Are-Self is one of the safest tools your child could use:

- **It runs on our school computer**—not in the cloud, not on a third-party server, not on someone else's hardware.
- **No internet connection needed**—after a one-time setup, the system is completely self-contained. Students' conversations never leave our school.
- **No accounts or logins**—students don't create usernames or passwords. There's no data profile being built about them.
- **No personal data collected**—Are-Self doesn't know students' names, addresses, grades, or family information unless they type it into a conversation (and I tell them not to).
- **No ads, no tracking, no analytics**—there's no company monitoring what students do or trying to sell them anything.
- **Open source**—the code is public and auditable, which means it's transparent and trustworthy.

All of the learning conversations stay on our school's server. I control who can access them, and I decide when to delete them.

### The Real Lesson: The Three Scipraxian Habits

This unit isn't just about learning with AI. The real goal is to help students develop three habits that will serve them in any classroom, job, or challenge they face:

1. **Inclusion**—Making space for different ideas and perspectives. In this unit, students learn that AI tools (and humans) can be wrong, and they have to think critically about what they hear.
2. **Humility**—Recognizing what you don't know and being willing to learn. Students will practice asking good questions and listening carefully.
3. **Inquiry**—Staying curious and digging deeper. Instead of accepting the first answer, students will learn to ask follow-up questions and test ideas.

These habits are what we're teaching. Are-Self is just the vehicle.

### How Can You Learn More?

If you'd like to know more about Are-Self, you can visit **are-self.com** or contact me directly. I'm happy to answer questions or show you how the tool works.

### What If You Have Concerns?

If you have any concerns about your child participating in this unit, please reach out to me. We can arrange alternative activities that let your child explore similar skills without using Are-Self. There's no penalty or awkwardness—I want every family to feel comfortable with what's happening in our classroom.

### You're Invited to Week 6

At the end of the six-week unit, we're hosting a presentation day where students will share what they learned and how they used Are-Self as a tool. I'd love for you to join us and see your child's work. Details will come closer to the date.

Thank you for being part of your child's learning. I'm excited about what we'll discover together.

Sincerely,

[TEACHER NAME]

## Spanish Translation (Key Points)

_Provide this as a separate page or attachment for families who prefer Spanish._

---

**CARTA A LAS FAMILIAS**

Estimado Padre/Madre o Tutor,

Le escribo para informarle sobre un emocionante proyecto de aprendizaje que comenzaremos en [GRADE/CLASS]. Durante las próximas seis semanas, su hijo/a trabajará con **Are-Self**, una herramienta de IA gratuita que lo ayudará a explorar matemáticas, ciencias, escritura, historia y otras materias de nuevas maneras.

### ¿Qué es Are-Self?

Are-Self es una herramienta de IA gratuita y de código abierto que funciona en la computadora de nuestra escuela. Es como un tutor inteligente que puede explicar conceptos, responder preguntas y ayudar a los estudiantes a resolver problemas. A diferencia de ChatGPT u otras herramientas de IA, Are-Self funciona completamente en el hardware de nuestra escuela. No se conecta a internet. No almacena nombres ni información personal. No muestra anuncios ni rastrea a los estudiantes. Está diseñado únicamente para el aprendizaje en el aula.

### ¿Qué harán los estudiantes?

Su hijo/a usará Are-Self en varias materias para:
- Hacer preguntas y obtener explicaciones de conceptos difíciles
- Hacer lluvia de ideas para escritura y proyectos
- Resolver problemas de matemáticas paso a paso
- Investigar eventos históricos y temas científicos
- Recibir retroalimentación sobre su pensamiento

Los estudiantes trabajarán en parejas o en grupos pequeños. Yo dirigiré todo el proceso y tendré control total sobre lo que Are-Self puede hacer y qué conversaciones se guardan.

### ¿Por qué es seguro?

Entiendo que pueda tener preguntas sobre IA en el aula. Aquí está por qué confío en que Are-Self es una de las herramientas más seguras que su hijo/a podría usar:

- **Funciona en la computadora de nuestra escuela**—no en la nube, no en un servidor de terceros.
- **No necesita conexión a internet**—después de una configuración inicial única, el sistema es completamente independiente.
- **Sin cuentas ni contraseñas**—los estudiantes no crean nombres de usuario. No se crea un perfil de datos sobre ellos.
- **No se recopila información personal**—Are-Self no sabe los nombres, direcciones ni calificaciones a menos que los estudiantes los escriban.
- **Sin anuncios, seguimiento ni análisis**—no hay ninguna empresa monitoreando lo que hacen los estudiantes.
- **Código abierto**—el código es público y verificable.

Todas las conversaciones de aprendizaje se mantienen en el servidor de nuestra escuela. Yo controlo quién puede acceder y cuándo eliminarlas.

### Las Tres Costumbres Scipraxianas

Este proyecto enseña tres hábitos que servirán a los estudiantes en cualquier aula o desafío:

1. **Inclusión**—Hacer espacio para diferentes ideas y perspectivas.
2. **Humildad**—Reconocer qué no saben y estar dispuesto a aprender.
3. **Indagación**—Permanecer curiosos y profundizar más.

Estos hábitos son lo que enseñamos. Are-Self es solo la herramienta.

### ¿Cómo puedo aprender más?

Si desea saber más sobre Are-Self, puede visitar **are-self.com** o contactarme directamente. Estoy feliz de responder preguntas.

### ¿Qué si tengo preocupaciones?

Si tiene preocupaciones sobre la participación de su hijo/a, póngase en contacto conmigo. Podemos organizar actividades alternativas. No hay penalización.

### Invitación al Día 6

Al final de las seis semanas, haremos una presentación donde los estudiantes compartirán lo que aprendieron. Los invito a unirse.

Sinceramente,
