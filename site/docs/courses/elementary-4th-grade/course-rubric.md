# Course Rubric: Are-Self in the 4th Grade Classroom

Learner: ______________________ &nbsp; Facilitator: ______________________ &nbsp; Date: __________

**Ladder (0→5 is growth):** 0 Blank · 1 Term Recognition · 2 Limited Awareness · 3 Applicable Awareness · 4 Synergetic · 5 Instructor Level
**(O)** *can I do it?* &nbsp; **(S)** *do I understand it?* &nbsp; — write a number 0–5 in each cell. All answers correct.

> Growth is the goal. A learner who climbs from **1 Term Recognition** to **3 Applicable Awareness** has learned more than one who sat at **4 Synergetic** without moving.
