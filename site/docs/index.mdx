# Are-Self Learn

**Why do for yourself what Are-Self can do for you?**

Free, open-source curricula for teachers, students, trainers, and anyone
who wants to understand how AI actually works — by building with one.

Every course is MIT licensed, worksheet-backed, and designed so a
ten-year-old with curiosity (or a grandparent with patience) can use it.
Every course is built on top of
[Are-Self](https://are-self.com), an open-source, neurologically-inspired
AI reasoning swarm engine that runs on hardware you already own.

## How these courses are built

Every course follows the same template — a structure designed so any teacher
can pick it up and any student can follow it:

**Standardized frontmatter** with full metadata — audience, subject, level,
duration, standards alignment, learning objectives, prerequisites.
[See our tags →](./tags-reference)

**Worksheets in every lesson.** Non-negotiable. Interactivity is a
first-class requirement, not a nice-to-have.

**Rubrics** for every assessed activity. Growth over perfection.

**Standards alignment** where applicable — Common Core, NGSS, ACM/IEEE CS.

**Video scripts and transcripts** for accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA target).

All content is MIT licensed. Fork it, translate it, remix it for your
school, your church, your community center, your corporate training room.
Credit is appreciated. Permission is already granted.

## The philosophy underneath

Are-Self Learn exists inside a philosophy called
[scipraxianism](https://scipraxian.org) — co-developed by Michael Clark
and Andrew Piper. At its simplest, it is three habits taught to children:
**inclusion**, **humility**, and **inquiry**. At its fullest, it is twelve
decision variables that every adult weighs on every meaningful call.

The storybook that introduces these ideas to ten-year-olds is free and
online: [Mira and the Are-Self](https://are-self.com/docs/storybook).

The full twelve variables: Inclusion · Humility · Inquiry · Fulfillment
or Happiness · Religion or Profit · Fun · Fear · Responsibility ·
Perseverance · Perception · Time · Permadeath.
